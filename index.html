What We Offer

Our Services

From cutting to mounting, we do it all so you don't have to lift a finger.

01
Custom Cutting & Polishing

We cut, shape, and polish the glass edges right at our Nairobi workshop. If you want an arch, round, or pebble mirror, we shape it by hand.

02
Fitting & Wall Mounting

Heavy mirrors are tricky to hang. Our guys bring the right brackets, drills, and wall plugs to mount your mirrors safely, switch on the LED wiring, and clean up after.

03
Custom Mirrored Furniture

We build console tables, bedside chests, and vanity desks clad in polished mirrors to give your room a bright, modern look.

04
Safe Delivery

Mirrors are fragile. We wrap them in thick foam, pack them on custom wooden frames, and deliver them in our own vans so they arrive in one piece.

How We Work

Simple 4-Step Process

From first text to finished installation — we keep it simple, honest, and straightforward.

01
WhatsApp Us

Send us a message with the design you want and rough sizes. We'll give you a price quote.

02
We Measure

For large or complex orders, we'll send a team to take final measurements at your place so it fits perfectly.

03
We Build It

We cut the mirror, polish the edges, assemble the frames, and wire the LEDs in our Nairobi workshop.

04
Delivery & Fitting

We deliver and mount it securely on your wall. We test the LEDs, wipe the glass clean, and leave your place tidy.
